our education

Music lessons

Our expert instructors guide students through a diverse range of musical genres, from classical to contemporary, fostering a deep appreciation for music's cultural and historical significance. Students are encouraged to explore their musical interests and develop their unique artistic voices.

Sports lessons

Through our comprehensive sports program, we aim to cultivate not only athletic prowess but also valuable life skills that will serve our students well beyond the field, court, or track. Under the guidance of our experienced coaches, students have the opportunity to explore a wide range of sports, from traditional favorites to emerging disciplines. Whether they're dribbling a soccer ball, shooting hoops on the basketball court, or perfecting their serve on the tennis court, students are encouraged to discover their passion for physical activity and competition.

Drawing lessons

Through our engaging art program, we aim to cultivate not only artistic skills but also a lifelong passion for visual storytelling and creative exploration. Under the guidance of our experienced instructors, students have the opportunity to explore a variety of drawing techniques, mediums, and styles. From graphite pencils to colored pastels, students learn to wield a diverse range of artistic tools to bring their imagination to life on paper.
